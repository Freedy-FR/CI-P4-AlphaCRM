Alpha CRM - Customer Management Instructions

Step 1: Create a New Customer

To create a new customer in Alpha CRM, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your Alpha CRM account.
  2. Go to the "Customers" section in the navigation menu.
  3. Click the "Add Customer" button.
  4. Fill in the customer's details, including name, contact information, and any additional information.
  5. Click the "Add" button to create the new customer record.

Step 2: Update Customer Details

To update customer details in Alpha CRM, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your Alpha CRM account.
  2. Go to the "Customers" section in the navigation menu.
  3. Find the customer whose details you want to update.
  4. Click on the customer's row to open their profile.
  5. Click the "Update" button to make changes to the customer's information.
  6. After making the necessary updates, click the "Update" button to apply the changes.

Step 3: Delete a Customer

To delete a customer in Alpha CRM, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your Alpha CRM account.
  2. Go to the "Customers" section in the navigation menu.
  3. Find the customer you want to delete.
  4. Click on the customer's row to open their profile.
  5. Click the "Delete" button.
  6. Confirm the deletion in the pop-up dialog.

Step 4: View Customer Details

To view customer details in Alpha CRM, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your Alpha CRM account.
  2. Go to the "Customers" section in the navigation menu.
  3. Click on the customer's name to open their profile.
  4. You can view all the information associated with the customer, including their contact details, interactions, and any notes.
